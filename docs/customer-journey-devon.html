Linnea Park

Principal Product Designer

Devon's valley is at Stage 7, not Stage 6. He doesn't need a Founder Digest — he needs a Curation Surface: a triage view with internal/share toggles, saved profiles, and bulk operations. Same data, different lens. Ship the dual-register schema and you can render either lens from one source of truth.

Mira Okonkwo

Brand & Design Director

The audit PDF is the artifact that gets emailed, printed, signed. It carries Devon's agency brand and the client's name. Treat it like a typeset deliverable, not a screen-dump. This is the surface where you graduate from "tool" to "agency infrastructure" — and where premium pricing lives.

Rishi Madan

Staff ML Engineer

Devon's run-history across clients is your most valuable signal. Anonymized, it tells you which finding-types matter across an industry (dental vs contractor vs e-com). That dataset becomes your pricing power: the system gets smarter as agencies use it. Build the multi-repo dashboard and the telemetry compounds.
